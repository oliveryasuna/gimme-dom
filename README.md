# Gimme DOM

A Vaadin 8 extension enabling developers to have more control over the web DOM.

## Why

The following is the true story to the best of my recollection of how Gimme DOM came to be.

One day I got fed up with Vaadin coming home late and not showing me the love I deserve.
So I decided to sit Vaadin down and have a chat. I asked her, "why don't you have click
listeners for labels? JavaFX does. Why can't you be like her, loving, caring, and
functional?" Vaadin didn't respond. I knew she was hiding something. Then, I remembered
that Vaadin is a piece of software, not a human being. But, like human beings, software
makes mistakes and always has room for improvement. The end. Bye-bye.

### "Gimme" - what does that mean?

As-in, "give me". Duh... I.e., "Give me more DOM." And my next project will be called
Gimme DOM NOW. Obviously, for those who don't know words, that means "Give me more DOM
[National Organization for Woman](https://now.org/)." Or maybe it is me who does not know
words, considering I am using the word "words" incorrectly and put "obviously" in the wrong
place... But, I digress with no further comments.
